Owl SMS: Refined Framework
1. Administrative Hub (System Level)
Focus: Infrastructure, security, and oversight.
System Dashboard: Overview of school-wide stats (total enrollment, revenue, active tickets).
User Management: Global "Super-Admin" control to add/enable/disable staff and guardian accounts.
Institution Settings: Manage sessions, terms, and school-wide grading scales.
Support Ticket System: Manage technical issues with statuses: Active, Resolved, Terminated.
Audit Trail: Detailed logs of user activities (filterable by date/user) for accountability.

2. Staff Portal (Academic & Operations)
Focus: Specialized roles for school management.
Principal: Teacher recruitment/onboarding, performance oversight, and high-level parent communication.
Bursar: Fee management, payment verification, and automated receipt generation.
Enrolment Officer: New student registration and student file management.
Teacher/Academic Staff:
Subject Management: Create subjects and link them to classrooms.
Class Management: Assign Form Teachers to specific arms (e.g., JSS1 Gold).
Academic Tools: Input scores, manage attendance, and set assignments.
Specialized Staff:
Nurse: Student health logs, allergy tracking, and medical incident reports.
Librarian: Book cataloging and borrowing history.

3. Student Portal (Learning & Engagement)
Focus: Self-service academic tracking.
Academic Profile: Current subjects, personalized timetable, and school calendar.
Assignment Center: Deadlines, reminders, file uploads, and graded feedback.
Performance Tracking: Termly scores and cumulative GPA.
CBT Practice: Self-paced mock exams with instant feedback/corrections (JAMB/WAEC prep style).

4. Guardian Portal (The "Home-School" Bridge)
